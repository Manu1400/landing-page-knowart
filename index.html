Frequently Asked Questions

01.

For User

Who will have the chance to benefit from your service?

Anyone with an internet connection, an ID proof, a bank account, a credit card or a debit card, a pinch of curiosity and a taste for new challenges.

Our service will be first geographically limited to the European citizens. So, please follow us on socials and/or register to our pioneer’s list to be kept up to date and to be noticed of our expansion into your part of the world.

02.

For User

Who will manage the artworks?

KnowArt will own 30% of the artworks and will be the one who manages the artworks’ property. It will be absolutely mandatory. We will take care of every single aspect, from the first transport from the artists to the insurance, from the storage to the shipping to the institutions, museums, galeries or rental clients.

03.

For User

How much will your service cost?

Overall, KnowArt will provide a very cost-effective service to its investors and will show a careful attention to the protection of its investors’ ROI. Please find the detailed supportive information below:

On the primary market, our investors’ transactions will be free of charges.

On the secondary market, our investors will have to cover a 1% fee on each transaction made (split evenly between buyer and seller)

04.

For Advertiser

What payment type will I be able to use on your platform?

We will accept instant debit and credit card payments. We cannot accept bank transfers because that would mean lock shares of an artwork for a long period of time, and we believe that it would be unfair for the other users.

05.

For Advertiser

Is there a minimum investment?

The only threshold is the value of one share, which can range from 10€/£8 up to 500€/£450.

06.

For Advertiser

How will I be able to register on FH?

The registration is going to be very easy. You can sign up using your email or any of your social account. However, we will need more information from you if you want to invest more than £500.
